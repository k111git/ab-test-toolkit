plot_power

 plot_power (simulation, added_lines=[], is_effect=True)

Takes simulation dict and plots the power over sample sizes Added lines: plot horizontal lines for additional sample size estimations, takes a list of dicts with sample_size=sample_size,label=label is_effect: True: there is an effect in reality, hence we are interested in power. False: we are interested in false positives.
